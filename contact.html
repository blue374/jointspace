Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any prior experience to start learning?

No! Our content is designed for absolute beginners. We start with the basics and gradually build up to more advanced topics.

What equipment do I need?

You can start learning with just a computer! Many of our demos run in your browser. For hands-on projects, we provide detailed parts lists with budget-friendly options.

Is JointSpace free?

Yes! All our basic tutorials, videos, and demos are completely free. We believe robotics education should be accessible to everyone.

Can I share my own projects?

Absolutely! We love showcasing student projects. Use the contact form above to submit your project for our community showcase.

How often is new content added?

We add new videos and tutorials every week! Follow us on social media or subscribe to our newsletter to stay updated.
